My Story

I hold a Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Mathematics from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi and a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Mathematics from University of Delhi. With a strong foundation in mathematics, statistics, and analytical thinking, I am passionate about leveraging data to solve complex business problems and drive informed decision-making.

My areas of interest include Data Science, Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. I have experience working with SQL, PostgreSQL, Python, and Data Visualization tools, and I am continuously expanding my expertise in technologies such as Snowflake, ETL pipelines, Docker, MongoDB,Pyspark and cloud-based data solutions. I enjoy transforming raw data into meaningful insights, building scalable data workflows, and applying analytical techniques to support business growth and innovation.

I am committed to continuous learning and aspire to build a successful career at the intersection of mathematics, data, and technology.

My jouney

I was introduced to the basics of programming during my school years up to Class 10. After completing my Class 12 education, I pursued a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Delhi. At that time, I was not aware of fields such as Data Science and Data Analytics that combine mathematics with programming.

During the second year of my bachelor's degree, I learned about careers that leverage both mathematical concepts and programming skills. Although I found the field interesting, I had not yet explored it in depth. After completing my bachelor's degree, I qualified for admission to IIT Delhi and decided to pursue an M.Sc. in Mathematics to further strengthen my academic foundation.

During my first semester at IIT Delhi, I began exploring Data Science and Analytics more seriously. The more I learned about the field, the more I realized how well it aligned with my interests and strengths in mathematics, problem-solving, and technology. This exploration gradually evolved into a clear career goal, and since then I have been actively building the skills and knowledge required to establish myself in this domain. That journey has brought me to where I am today, pursuing a career in Data Science and Analytics.
